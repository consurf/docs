Introduction

Welcome to the Sneakon Documentation page! This guide will help you get started with Sneakon, a revolutionary browser designed to enhance your online experience.

Installation

Follow these steps to install Sneakon:

  • Download the Sneakon installer from the official website.
  • Extract the ZIP.
  • Launch `SneakonSearch.exe`.

Features

Sneakon comes packed with amazing features to improve your browsing experience:

  • Dark Mode: Use a sleek dark theme that's easy on the eyes.
  • SneakShop: Explore and purchase exclusive themes, extensions, and more.
  • Bug Reporting: Help us improve Sneakon by reporting any bugs you encounter.
  • High Speed: Enjoy lightning-fast browsing with our optimized engine.
  • Efficiency: Uses less RAM than 90% of browsers while still having an even faster speed.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions about Sneakon:

  • Q: How do I enable Dark Mode? A: Dark mode is already enabled as of V1.5. Ensure your browser is up to date.
  • Q: Where can I report a bug? A: Use the 'Report a Bug' button in the Sneakon menu and fill out the form.
  • Q: Can I get games? A: Yes! Visit SneakShop to explore games and more!
  • Q: Is Sneakon secure? A: Absolutely! Sneakon offers advanced security features to keep your browsing safe.
